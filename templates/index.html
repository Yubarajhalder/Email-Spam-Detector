About Spam Detection

Understanding the problem and our ML approach

What is Spam?

Unsolicited, irrelevant, or fraudulent messages sent in bulk — often containing scams, phishing links, or unwanted promotions.

What is Ham?

Legitimate, wanted messages from people or organizations you trust — personal emails, work updates, and genuine notifications.

Why It Matters

Spam costs billions annually, spreads malware, and erodes trust. Automated detection protects users and organizations at scale.

How ML Works

Text is converted to numerical features via TF-IDF, then classified by a trained Logistic Regression model.
